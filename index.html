In My Own Words

Most people's default work habits are reactive — you respond to what comes in, you write what comes to mind, you do things the way you've always done them. AI forces you to break that pattern. To use it well, you have to articulate exactly what you're trying to accomplish. You can't think vaguely at an AI tool. The AI doesn't do the thinking — it makes the thinking visible so you can pressure-test it.
